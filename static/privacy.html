Privacy Policy

Event Horizon — Cleanlystudio

Overview

Event Horizon is an interactive WebGL experience. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data necessary to deliver the experience.

Data collected

  • Analytics: Anonymous page views and performance metrics to improve the experience. No personal identification.
  • Companion sync: If you connect a phone via QR code, a temporary real-time channel is created. No data is stored after the session ends.
  • Local storage: GPU performance profile and language preference are saved locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. Local storage is used solely for performance optimization and user preferences.

Third-party services

  • Supabase: Real-time companion sync (data is ephemeral, not stored).
  • Vercel: Hosting and analytics (anonymous).

Your rights

You can clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser's local storage. No account or personal data is collected, so there is nothing to delete on our end.
